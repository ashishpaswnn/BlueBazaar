Cancellation and Refund Policy

At BlueBazaar.store, we strive to ensure a smooth and satisfying shopping experience. Below are our policies regarding order cancellations and refunds.

1. Order Cancellation

  • You can cancel your order within 6 hours of placing it or before it is shipped, whichever is earlier.
  • To cancel your order, please email us at support@bluebazaar.store or contact our customer support.
  • Once the order is shipped, it cannot be cancelled.

2. Return & Replacement

  • Products are eligible for return/replacement only if:
    • They are damaged or defective
    • You received the wrong item
  • Returns must be requested within 3 days of delivery.
  • The product must be unused and in its original condition with packaging.
  • We may request photos/videos for verification.
